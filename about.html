Who We Are

More Than a Juice Bar

Zestify is a celebration of nature's finest offerings. We're not just a juice bar — we're a movement. A community of people who believe that what you put in your body truly matters, and that the best fuel comes from the earth itself.

Founded in 2018 in the heart of Ahmedabad, we've grown from a tiny stall with six recipes to a beloved destination with over 50 freshly crafted drinks, each one telling a story of the farm it came from.

The Journey

How Zestify Began

2018

The First Squeeze

Founder Riya Patel started Zestify with a small cart outside Law Garden, Ahmedabad, with just 6 juice recipes and a handwritten menu. Lines formed on day one.

2019

Our First Store

After a viral Instagram post, we opened our first physical store on CG Road with 20 recipes, a cozy space, and a loyal community of health enthusiasts.

2021

Farm Partnerships

We forged direct partnerships with 12 local farms across Gujarat and Maharashtra to ensure our fruits are picked at peak ripeness — just for you.

2024

50+ Recipes & Growing

Today, Zestify serves over 12,000 happy customers with 50+ recipes, seasonal specials, and a commitment to zero-waste, eco-friendly packaging.

🎯
